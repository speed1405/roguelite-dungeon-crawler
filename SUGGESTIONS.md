# Suggestions

Thank you for playing Roguelite Dungeon Crawler! We welcome your feedback and suggestions to make the game better.

## How to Submit Suggestions

Please feel free to submit suggestions by:
- Opening an issue on GitHub
- Creating a pull request with your proposed changes
- Contacting the project maintainers

## Suggestion Categories

### Gameplay Features
- New biomes or levels
- Additional enemy types
- Power-ups or items
- Character abilities
- Difficulty adjustments

### Improvements
- Performance optimizations
- Visual enhancements
- UI/UX improvements
- Sound and music
- Controls and accessibility

### Bug Reports
- Gameplay bugs
- Collision detection issues
- Rendering problems
- Performance issues

### General Feedback
- Overall game experience
- Balance suggestions
- Quality of life improvements

## Current Suggestions

### Gameplay Features

#### Power-ups and Items
1. **Health Potions** - Consumable items that restore a portion of player health, dropped by enemies or found in the dungeon
2. **Temporary Power-ups**
   - Attack Damage Boost (increase damage by 50% for 30 seconds)
   - Speed Boost (increase movement speed by 30% for 20 seconds)
   - Invincibility Shield (temporary immunity to damage for 10 seconds)
   - Attack Range Extension (increase attack range by 50% for 25 seconds)
3. **Permanent Upgrades** - Collectible items that persist for the current run
   - Max Health Increase (+25 HP)
   - Damage Increase (+5 damage per attack)
   - Speed Increase (+10% movement speed)
   - Attack Cooldown Reduction (-0.1 seconds)

#### Progression Systems
4. **Meta-progression** - Unlock permanent upgrades between runs
   - Currency system (collect gold/souls from defeated enemies)
   - Unlock starting bonuses (start with +20 HP, +5 damage, etc.)
   - Unlock new character classes or abilities
5. **Level-up System** - Gain experience from defeating enemies, level up to choose upgrades
6. **Achievement System** - Track and reward player accomplishments
   - "Speed Runner" - Complete 5 levels in under 5 minutes
   - "Survivor" - Reach level 10 without taking damage
   - "Monster Hunter" - Defeat 100 enemies in a single run

#### Combat Enhancements
7. **Special Abilities per Class**
   - Warrior: Shield Block (reduce damage by 75% for 3 seconds, cooldown 15s)
   - Mage: Fireball (ranged projectile attack, cooldown 8s)
   - Rogue: Dash (quick movement burst, cooldown 5s)
   - Paladin: Healing Aura (restore 20 HP, cooldown 20s)
8. **Critical Hits** - Random chance to deal double damage
9. **Combo System** - Chain attacks for bonus damage
10. **Area of Effect (AoE) Attacks** - Hit multiple enemies at once

#### Enemy Variations
11. **Boss Encounters** - Special boss enemies at the end of certain levels (every 5 levels)
12. **Elite Enemies** - Stronger variants with unique abilities
   - Regenerating enemies that heal over time
   - Splitting enemies that divide when defeated
   - Summoning enemies that spawn additional monsters
13. **Enemy Behaviors**
   - Ranged enemies that attack from distance
   - Fleeing enemies that run away when low on health
   - Berserker enemies that get faster/stronger when damaged

### Improvements

#### Visual Enhancements
14. **Minimap** - Small map in the corner showing explored areas
15. **Visual Effects**
   - Screen shake on critical hits or taking damage
   - Better particle effects for different attack types
   - Death animations for enemies
   - Biome-specific ambient effects (falling leaves in Forest, embers in Lava)
16. **Health Bar for Enemies** - Display enemy health above their heads
17. **Damage Numbers** - Floating numbers showing damage dealt/taken

#### UI/UX Improvements
18. **Pause Menu** - Ability to pause the game and see current stats/controls
19. **Settings Menu**
   - Volume controls for sound effects and music
   - Graphics quality options
   - Control remapping
20. **Tutorial/Help System** - Brief tutorial for first-time players
21. **Better Death Screen** - Show run statistics (enemies defeated, time survived, furthest level)
22. **Tooltips** - Hover information for abilities, items, and stats

#### Performance and Quality of Life
23. **Save System** - Save progress mid-run to continue later
24. **High Score Leaderboard** - Track and display top scores
25. **Run Statistics** - Detailed stats tracking (damage dealt, damage taken, enemies killed per type)
26. **Seed System** - Allow players to input seeds for specific dungeon layouts
27. **Daily Challenge** - Special dungeon with predetermined seed that changes daily

### Sound and Music
28. **Background Music** - Different tracks for each biome
29. **Sound Effects**
   - Attack sounds (different per character class)
   - Enemy hit/death sounds (different per enemy type)
   - Footstep sounds
   - Level completion sound
   - Ambient biome sounds (water dripping in caves, wind in ice biome)

### Accessibility
30. **Colorblind Mode** - Alternative color schemes for better visibility
31. **Difficulty Settings** - Easy/Normal/Hard modes with adjusted enemy stats
32. **Mouse Controls** - Click-to-move and click-to-attack options
33. **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Quick keys for common actions
34. **Text Size Options** - Adjustable UI text size

### New Content
35. **Additional Biomes**
   - Ruins (ancient temple theme)
   - Underwater Cavern (ocean theme)
   - Corrupted Forest (dark magic theme)
36. **Weather Effects** - Rain in swamp, snow in ice cave, etc.
37. **Environmental Hazards**
   - Lava pools in lava biome (damage over time)
   - Poison gas in swamp
   - Ice patches (reduce movement speed)
   - Spike traps
38. **Interactive Objects**
   - Chests containing items/power-ups
   - Breakable walls revealing secret areas
   - Teleporters between rooms
   - Healing shrines

### Multiplayer Ideas
39. **Local Co-op** - Two players sharing the same screen
40. **Online Leaderboards** - Compare scores with other players globally
41. **Ghost Replay** - Watch replays of top-scoring runs
